About.

I'm Ethan Pacifico, a photographer working in street, documentary, and environmental portraiture.

My interest is in everyday life — ordinary moments, people in their element, and the way a person's surroundings say something about who they are. Most of my work is done on foot, in public, with a single camera.

Portraits are made on location rather than in a studio — at someone's workplace, in their neighbourhood, in the rooms they actually live in. The setting is always part of the subject.
